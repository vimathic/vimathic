VIMATHIC is free, ad-free, telemetry-free, and open-source. There are no premium tiers, no in-app purchases, no analytics tracking you, and no accounts to upsell. The project exists because mathematical visualization is worth building, and because the result might be useful to someone else.

If you find VIMATHIC useful and want to support continued development, any of the following helps directly. None of it is expected — the project is and will remain free.

## Other ways that aren't money

Money helps, but isn't the only useful form of support:

- **Star the repository** — [github.com/vimathic/vimathic](https://github.com/vimathic/vimathic). It increases visibility, which helps the project reach more people who might be interested.
- **Share with someone who might like it** — a VJ friend, a teacher, a fractal-curious music producer, a maths student. Word-of-mouth is how this kind of project finds its audience.
- **Report bugs or edge cases** — open an issue on GitHub with browser, OS, and what went wrong. Bug reports with reproducers are gold.
- **Contribute a MIDI controller mapping** — if your controller sends standard CC and you've worked out a useful default mapping, contribute it as `documents/midi-<controller>.md`. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](https://github.com/vimathic/vimathic/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) on GitHub for the format.
- **Fix a typo or clarify documentation** — small pull requests improving wording, accuracy, or examples are always welcome.

## What the support goes toward

There are no formal goals or milestones, but in rough order of priority:

1. Keeping VIMATHIC running and maintained — bug fixes, browser compatibility, security patches
2. Implementing the post-launch roadmap (new formulas, new shaders, new features) after the Phase 0-2 stability period concludes
3. Server costs (currently low — Cloudflare Pages is free, but custom domains and email services have small recurring costs)
4. Time invested in continued development — VIMATHIC is currently maintained by one person in their spare time

## A note on expectations

Contributions are appreciated but never expected. VIMATHIC is given to the world under [BUSL-1.1](https://github.com/vimathic/vimathic/blob/main/LICENSE.txt) (auto-converting to GPL v3 in 2031) regardless of how much support comes in. The project doesn't track who has or hasn't contributed and doesn't gate features behind donations.

Thank you for considering it — and for using VIMATHIC at all.
